Planned Giving
Your donation helps families and individuals in our community.
Ways to Plan Your Support to The Place
Monthly Giving
By joining Hearts of Impact, you become an integral part of our efforts to foster a thriving, resilient community. Together, we can make a lasting difference in the lives of our neighbors. Click this link to join now!
Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Funds
Do you love giving and need a great tax deduction? Increase your gift to The Place with one simple strategy for valuable financial benefits. Your donation can make a larger impact by donating long-term appreciated securities, including stocks, bonds, and mutual funds directly to The Place. Utilize one of the most tax-smart ways to give and reduce future capital gains through donating these assets directly to The Place. For more information, please contact Nicole Morgan at nicole@theplacega.org
In-Kind Donations
Donations can be made in-person at our admin office located at 2550 The Place Cir. We also have an Amazon Wishlist at this link that is updated with our current needs.
Tribute Gifts
Are you looking for an impactful way to support The Place’s mission? Tribute gifts help you honor a special loved one or leave a lasting memory. This giving option also provides a great way to commemorate a joyous occasion or say ‘Thank You’ to a business client. With your gift, your recipient will receive a personalized note from The Place sharing about your tribute gift. We look forward to partnering with you on this wonderful and meaningful donation opportunity. For more information about tribute gifts, please contact Nicole Morgan at nicole@theplacega.org
IRA Distributions
Are you 70 ½ or older and looking for an IRA distribution option that is tax-free and helps your community? By donating funds directly to The Place, you can meet your required minimum IRA distribution with no taxes. This option can potentially give donors larger giving capacity than contributing cash or other assets. IRA distributions are a great way to support The Place. Would you like to engage with The Place through IRA distributions? Please contact Nicole Morgan at nicole@theplacega.org
Employee Matching Gifts
Does your company have an employee charitable matching program? Employee Matching Gifts are an easy way to double or triple your donation. Matching gift programs show employees that their company not only encourages charitable giving but is committed to making their donations go further. Enter your company’s name in the Employee Match tool to see if your company participates.
Donor Advised Funds
Looking for a way to simplify your giving to The Place? Do you regularly give to The Place? You might benefit from streamlining your giving through a donor advised fund. A donor advised fund (DAF) gives an immediate tax deduction along with potentially reducing capital gains taxes and estate taxes. You get to recommend grants from the fund over time. You can contribute as often as you like. DAF’s provide donors with a flexible way to give to The Place and a tax-savvy method for setting aside philanthropic giving. Are you interested in contributing to The Place through a DAF? Contact Nicole Morgan at nicole@theplacega.org
