Court Mandated Community Service
Court Mandated / Community Service / Pre-Trial / Attorney Suggested
​All court-mandated community service hours must be in person and on-site at either location of The Place.
We do NOT offer remote/work from home for community service.
Orientation is no longer required. Please complete the online application and upload your ID and charges. Minimum age for court-mandated community service is 18 years old.
A copy of a photo ID, charge(s), or letter from an attorney listing charges and number of required community service hours is REQUIRED before acceptance of application.
