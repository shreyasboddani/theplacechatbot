Summer Hunger Is Real. Together, We Can Make a Difference
Summer is a season of sunshine, family vacations, and children enjoying a well-deserved break from school. But for many families in our community, summer also brings a difficult reality: when school is out, access to regular, nutritious meals becomes much harder.
For many children, school breakfast and lunch programs provide consistent, healthy meals during the school year. Children can miss up to 10 meals per week during the summer months. When those meals are no longer available, parents and caregivers are left trying to stretch already limited grocery budgets to cover additional meals every day.
At The Place, we see this need firsthand. Throughout the summer, more families turn to our pantry for help because they simply need a little extra support to keep healthy food on the table.
Every child deserves a summer filled with fun, friendships, and opportunities to grow - not the uncertainty of wondering where their next meal will come from.
That's why our mission doesn't take a summer vacation.
Every week, generous donors, dedicated volunteers, and caring community partners help us provide food to children, families, seniors, and neighbors facing food insecurity. Their kindness allows us to offer hope along with every bag of groceries distributed.
But we can't do it alone.
