What is The Cottage?
We created The Cottage to serve goal-oriented young women between the ages of 18 and 24 who are experiencing housing instability.
The Cottage
THE MISSION:
To develop and empower confident, independent, self-sufficient, young women
ABOUT:
The Cottage is a residence for goal-oriented young women ages 18-24. The Cottage aims to ensure a safe, stable, and supportive home environment for young women who are experiencing housing instability.
