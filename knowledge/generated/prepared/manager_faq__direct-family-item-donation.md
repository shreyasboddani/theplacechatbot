# I have a specific item that I want to go directly to a family in need. What should I do?

Source ID: direct-family-item-donation
Source type: manager_faq
Approval status: approved

## Staff-approved answer

Thank you for your generosity. You may email paige@theplacega.org to inquire whether we currently have a family in need of these items. Unfortunately, the timing is not always aligned, and due to limited storage space, we are unable to hold items until a family becomes available. However, we can provide vouchers for families to obtain needed items from our thrift store. We kindly ask that you consider donating your item(s) to the thrift store. This allows families to access items as they are needed while also helping fund our programs and services.
