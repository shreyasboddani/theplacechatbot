FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE REQUEST/​SOLICITUD DE ASISTENCIA FINANCIERA
To request assistance, please fill out the information below.Para solicita asistencia, por favor llene la información de abajo.
Eligibility Requirements:– Photo ID– Current, Signed Lease– Utility Bills (Electric, Water, & Gas)– Proof of Income– 30-day Bank Statement
The applicant must live in Forsyth or Dawson County, GA. If the primary residence is not within Forsyth or Dawson County, please call the United Way at 211 (toll free) for an agency that serves your area.El solicitante debe vivir en el condado de Forsyth o Dawson, GA. Si la residencia principal no está dentro de Forsyth o el condado de Dawson, llame a United Way al 211 (sin cargo) para buscar una agencia que preste servicios en su área.
What to expect after completing application:
A member of our team will reach out to you within 3 to 5 business days
We will request additional documentation
IF we can assist, a member of our team will notify you
